Who We Are

About Prime Central

Prime Central Air Conditioning Co. L.L.C is an electromechanical company based in Abu Dhabi, engaged in providing total quality solutions to the electro-mechanical and air conditioning field. We have been in the business since 2000 and have gained reputation and trust from our valuable satisfied customers and clients.

Prime undertakes various contracts for the design, supply and installation of air-conditioning and electro-mechanical systems for all commercial buildings, residential buildings, industrial warehouses, villas and marine works. We do the best and quality works as well as provide Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC).

Our management team comprises of the Managing Director who has been in the same field for over 35 years in various levels in the UAE, and has undertaken total project management responsibilities for highly reputed corporate and top-end individual clients. The team also includes Project Managers, Project Engineers, Administrators, Workshop Foremen, Site Supervisors and Skilled Technicians.
